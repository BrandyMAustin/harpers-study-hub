    💡 ADHD Focus Tips

    Body Doubling

    Study with a friend (even on video call) — just having someone nearby helps your brain stay on track!

    Task Chunking

    Break big assignments into tiny pieces. Instead of "study for test," try "review 5 flashcards" then "do 3 practice problems."

    Movement Breaks

    During your 5-min break, do jumping jacks, dance to a song, or walk around. Movement recharges your focus!

    Reward Yourself

    After each Pomodoro session, give yourself a small reward — a snack, a quick game, or 5 min of your favorite show!
